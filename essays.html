Hello world

27 July 2024

No, this is not an essay about how I wrote my first computer program. Instead, this is a greeting essay for the beginning of a new journey - me being publicly active and sharing my thoughts and progress.

Since my freshman year of college, I have always heard from people I admire to write more publicly but never had the courage to do it. I have always been a very private person with no social media accounts (not even Instagram) and stayed away from sharing my opinions publicly on any platform.

But finally, this is the beginning of my final year of college, and I am getting started with it. A lot of people talk about building and learning in public to build a personal brand and to get opportunities. I don't know what will happen or if anything will happen at all. I don't have any expectations or goals to reach, nor am I chasing anything. I will write just for the sake of it.

I am here to be myself and discover new ideas. Who knows, I might end up meeting some really cool people and making connections for life. Sometimes, I might say something cool and sometimes something really stupid, but never mind; the goal is just to be more authentic and be myself.

And this is my first piece of writing online, being vocal about my thoughts. It's a really nice feeling to see my thoughts take shape on a white canvas. It's very satisfying.

Hope you enjoyed this one! Until next time, stay curious, and keep building.🚀

Cheers, Ayush!
