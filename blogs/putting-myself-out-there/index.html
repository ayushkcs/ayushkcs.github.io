Putting Myself Out There

17 Jan, 2025

Finally, after almost four years of postponing, I’ve gathered the courage to put myself out there and start writing publicly about the things that fascinate and intrigue me.

Since my freshman year of college, I’ve often heard from people I admire that I should write more openly. Yet, I never had the courage to do it. I’ve been a very private person, with no active social media presence, and have stayed away from sharing my opinions publicly on any platform.

But now, I’ve decided to embrace the challenge. I’m not setting grand expectations or chasing specific goals. I’ll simply write for the joy of expressing myself. I’m here to be myself, fully and authentically. Who knows? Along the way, I might meet some amazing people and build meaningful connections. Some days, I’ll say something thought-provoking; other days, maybe not so much. And that’s okay—the point is to enjoy the process and stay true to myself.

As someone new to writing, I know there will be mistakes, but I’m eager to learn and improve. I genuinely look forward to your feedback and thoughts as I grow through this journey.

This is my first piece of writing online, where I’m being vocal about my thoughts. It’s incredibly satisfying to see them take shape on a blank canvas. There’s a certain thrill in bringing ideas to life through words.

Lastly, this serves as a reminder to myself—to stay authentic, to not let algorithms dictate my voice, and to keep it real.

I hope you enjoyed this one! Until next time, stay curious, and keep building.

Cheers :)
